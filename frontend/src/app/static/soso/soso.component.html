SOSO is a holistic approach to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all in lines with the Sustainable Development Goals - 6 of the United Nations.

Vision

The aim of Swachha Odisha Sustha Odisha is to improve the standard of living of the people of rural Odisha and to ensure that all villages in Odisha are clean and green villages.

Objectives
  • Ensure safe sanitation facilities for all the people of rural Odisha through access to Individual Household Latrines (IHHLs)
  • Ensure availability of clean drinking water for every citizen of Odisha
  • Make all the villages in Odisha Open-Defecation Free
  • Eliminate the use of plastic bags across Odisha
  • Promote solid and liquid waste management
