Rural Sanitation in Odisha

The Swachha Bharat Mission was launched on 2nd October, 2014 with the objective to accelerated the efforts to achieve universal sanitation coverage and to put focus on sanitation and thereby achieve a Swachh Bharat by 2019, as a fitting tribute to Mahatma Gandhi on his 150th birth anniversary. Since April 2017, the SBM(Gramin) is being implemented in the state of Odisha under the aegis of the Department of Panchayati Raj and Drinking Water, Govt of Odisha. The program is integral part of the Swachha Odisha Sustha Odisha (SOSO) initiative by Government of Odisha which is a holistic approach to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all in lines with the Sustainable Development Goals - 6 of the United Nations.
